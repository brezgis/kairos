# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-16_

Kairos is a personal, single-user analytics project operated by its owner for
their own use. It is not a commercial product or a service offered to the public.

## What data is processed

Kairos retrieves data from third-party services that the owner explicitly
authorizes, which may include:

- Oura Ring health metrics (sleep, readiness, activity, heart rate, SpO2,
  stress, workouts, and related data)
- Calendar events
- Music listening history
- Weather data for the owner's location

## Whose data

Only the data of the owner who operates this application. Kairos does not
collect, process, or store data belonging to any other person.

## How data is used and stored

Data is used solely for the owner's personal analytics. It is stored privately
in infrastructure controlled by the owner. It is not sold, shared, or disclosed
to third parties.

## Third-party services

Kairos accesses third-party APIs (such as Oura) using credentials the owner
authorizes. Use of those services is governed by their respective privacy
policies and terms. Kairos is not affiliated with or endorsed by them.

## Revoking access

The owner can revoke Kairos's access to any connected service at any time
through that service's account settings (for Oura, via the Oura account's
connected applications).
